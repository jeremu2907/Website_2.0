Project Yumiya Try out this project! Request for alpha test access

I created a productivity application that aims to strengthen users' time management by highlighting daily events, providing a quick note-taking space, and providing insights to potential unexpected risks

This program is written in ReactJS, ExpressJS and integrates the Google Calendar, Google Maps, OpenWeatherMap APIs, as well as MongoDB

Yumiya ( 弓矢 ) in Japanese means bow and arrow. To have the most beautiful and perfect shots, the archer must always be aware of the surrounding and environment around them. In a similar manner, this application provides all the necessary environmental factors to the users so that they can plan the most optimal and efficient schedule for the day
